# **From Vision to Collective Action**
### **A Framework for Shared Knowledge**



Systemic change starts not with ownership, but with a blueprint for **belonging**. Too often, powerful ideas are either gated or co-opted. This framework is designed to prevent both.

I want these ideas to remain accessible in the **public domain**, serving as shared knowledge rather than being appropriated for unintended agendas. My aim is to shape an action-oriented framework that calls for collective engagement and supports the dignity and empowerment of marginalized communities.

To move our vision from concept to collective action, I use a four-layer **Progression Map** that acts as a living invitation:
